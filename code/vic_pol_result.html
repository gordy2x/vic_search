Methods

All analyses were conducted in R version 4.0.3.

Data

We classified racial appearance …. We combined “ABORIGINAL”,“BOTH TSI & ABORIGINAL”,“TORRES STRAIT ISLANDER (TSI)” into “ABORIGINAL and/or TSI” and coded “NO INFO PROVIDED”, “NOT STATED / UNKNOWN” as Missing Unknown sex was coded to Missing Recruit (small numbers) combined into Const Coded search reason by combining to weapons, drugs and graffiti We combined finding package or thing due to small numbers We truncated age at 80 Find is any of PROHIBITED.WEAPONS, DANGEROUS.ARTICLES, ,CONTROLLED.WEAPONS,.ARTICLES,GRAFFITI.IMPLEMENTS, VOLATILE.SUBSTANCES.TYPES We removed any with SEARCH_WARRANT or DPCSA_S_81_SRCH_WITH_WARRANT, as well as Person.Reports = No Person data

There was a large amount of missing data in the model variables, with Racial Appearance having 3404 (18%) missing values, search reason having 998 (5%) missing values, and Age and Sex less than 1% missing (Figure XX attached).
